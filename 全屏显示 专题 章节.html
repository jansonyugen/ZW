n. 困难；困境

confront

v. 〔问题、困难等〕使遇到，使面对；勇敢地面对，正视

009

benefit

v. 受益，得益；有益于，有利于

considerably from 从……中获益匪浅


010

encourage / encouragement

鼓励的话

stimulate sb to do sth 

激励某人做某事

引起兴趣/期望

调动积极性

supportsb in (doing) sth

n. 代替者

013

bear/keep in mind 

牢记于心


e.g.

  • I’m writing to thank you for your kind help when…

    我写信是想感谢你在我……之时对我的热心帮助。

  • I am writing to express my sincere gratitude for (heartfelt thanks to / warmest appreciation of) … while …

    此次写信是为了感谢……时候……

  • I am writing to express my great appreciation for your timely help and assistance when I had difficulties in doing sth

    我写信是想感谢您在我……遇到困难时给予了及时的帮助。

  • I would like to express my gratitude / appreciation of all your kind help.

    我对您的所有热心帮助表示感谢。

  • Thank you for sharing your experience of… with me.

    感谢您跟我分享有关……的经验。

  • I’d appreciate it if...

    如果……，我会非常感激。

  • I would like to say a sincere thank you to everyone who has helped and supported me.

    我要对所有帮助和支持过我的人表示由衷的感谢。

  • You supported me enormously.

    你给了我极大的支持。

  • My interest in art was stimulated by you.

    我对艺术的兴趣是您激发的。

  • There can be no substitute for a good teacher like you, who can impart facts and theories, as well as appraise and encourage his students.

    没有什么可以替代像您这样的好老师，不仅能传道授业，还会评估并鼓励自己的学生。

  • Your…will make a crucial difference, and I couldn’t be more grateful for your…

    你的……将对我有很大影响，我十分感谢……。

  • Having a good teacher has made all the difference for me (= had an important influence).

    有个好老师对我来说影响很大。

  • Therefore, I would like to thank you again for all that you have done.

    所以再次感谢您为我所做的一切。

  • And I will bear / keep in mind what you have done…and try to do… in the future.

    我将铭记……，并在将来努力……。
